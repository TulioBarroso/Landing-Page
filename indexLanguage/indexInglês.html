About Us

We are a company that provides national road and air transportation. We have our own fleet of light, medium, and heavy vehicles to meet different cargo configurations. We also have a 1000m² warehouse offering general storage services. We believe that care and responsibility with deadlines make all the difference; we always aim to surprise our customers' clients — that’s our policy. Your delivery, that's our goal.

We have ANVISA authorizations

Focusing on the market for medicines and related health products, we have ANVISA authorization for the transport and storage of:

» Medicines for health.
» Controlled medicines (Ordinance 344/1998).
» Sanitizers.
» Cosmetics.
